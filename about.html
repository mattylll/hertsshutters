About HertsShutters

Your Trusted Window Treatment Experts Since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, HertsShutters began as a family-run business with a simple mission: to bring premium quality window treatments to homes across Hertfordshire. What started in a small workshop in Harpenden has grown into the county's most trusted name in shutters and blinds.

Our founder, James Mitchell, a master craftsman with over 25 years of experience in joinery, recognized a gap in the market for truly bespoke, high-quality window treatments that combined traditional craftsmanship with modern functionality. His vision was to create a company that would not just sell products, but transform homes.

Today, from our showroom on Piggottshill Lane in Harpenden, we serve thousands of satisfied customers across Hertfordshire. We've maintained our commitment to quality, employing only skilled craftsmen and using the finest materials, while embracing innovation in design and technology.

Every shutter and blind we install carries our promise of excellence, backed by our comprehensive 10-year warranty and ongoing support. We're proud to be part of the Hertfordshire community, contributing to the beauty and comfort of homes from St Albans to Stevenage, Watford to Welwyn Garden City.
