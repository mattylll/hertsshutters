Discover Elegant Café Style Shutters

Café style shutters offer the perfect balance of privacy and natural light, providing street-level discretion whilst maintaining the bright, airy feel that makes Hertfordshire homes so inviting.

As specialists in café style shutters, we understand that ground-floor privacy is essential for comfortable living, especially in busy areas like St Albans high street or Harpenden's residential roads. Our half-height shutters provide complete privacy at eye level whilst allowing natural light to flood in from above, creating the perfect solution for street-facing windows.

The timeless appeal of café style shutters lies in their versatility and understated elegance. Originally inspired by French bistros where privacy was needed at table height, these shutters have become a sophisticated choice for modern homes, offering practical benefits without compromising on style.

What sets our café style shutters apart is the precision craftsmanship and attention to detail. Each installation is custom-made to your exact window specifications, ensuring perfect proportions and flawless operation that enhances your home's character while providing lasting functionality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What height should café style shutters be?

Typically, café style shutters cover the lower third to half of your window, usually around 60-100cm from the windowsill. We'll recommend the perfect height based on your specific privacy needs and window proportions.

Can café style shutters be combined with other treatments?

Absolutely! Many clients combine café style shutters with blinds or curtains in the upper section, or later add full-height panels for complete flexibility.

How much do café style shutters cost?

Café style shutters typically cost £150-400 per square metre. Since they cover less area than full-height shutters, they offer excellent value whilst providing essential privacy and style benefits.

Are they suitable for all window types?

Yes, we can create café style shutters for most window types including bay windows, sash windows, and casement windows. Each installation is custom-made to fit perfectly.
