2. Services

2.1 Service Description

HertsShutters provides:

  • Supply and installation of window shutters and blinds
  • Free consultation and measurement services
  • Custom manufacturing of window treatments
  • Professional installation services
  • After-sales support and maintenance

Our services are available throughout Hertfordshire and surrounding areas. Additional charges may apply for locations outside our standard service area.

3. Quotations and Pricing

3.1 Quotations

  • All quotations are valid for 30 days from the date of issue
  • Quotations are based on measurements taken at the time of survey
  • Prices quoted are inclusive of VAT unless otherwise stated
  • Quotations include standard installation unless specified otherwise

3.2 Price Changes

We reserve the right to adjust prices if:

  • Specifications change after the quotation is accepted
  • Additional work is required not included in the original quote
  • Access difficulties or structural issues are discovered during installation

4. Ordering and Payment

4.1 Order Confirmation

Orders become binding when:

  1. You accept our quotation in writing or verbally
  2. We receive your deposit payment
  3. We issue an order confirmation

4.2 Payment Terms

  • 50% deposit required upon order confirmation
  • Balance due upon completion of installation
  • Payment accepted via bank transfer, credit/debit card, or cheque
  • Late payments may incur interest at 8% above Bank of England base rate

4.3 Custom Orders

All shutters and blinds are made to measure. Once production begins, orders cannot be cancelled or modified without potential charges.

5. Delivery and Installation

5.1 Lead Times

  • Standard lead time: 6-8 weeks from order confirmation
  • Express service available for additional charge
  • Lead times are estimates and may vary due to circumstances beyond our control

5.2 Installation

Installation includes:

  • Professional fitting by qualified installers
  • Removal of packaging materials
  • Basic cleaning of work area
  • Demonstration of product operation

5.3 Customer Responsibilities

You must ensure:

  • Clear access to windows and work areas
  • Removal of valuable or fragile items from work areas
  • An adult is present during installation
  • Adequate parking is available for our vehicles

6. Warranty and Guarantees

6.1 Product Warranty

  • 10-year warranty on all shutters against manufacturing defects
  • 5-year warranty on blinds against manufacturing defects
  • 2-year warranty on motorized components
  • Warranties are non-transferable

6.2 Installation Guarantee

We guarantee our installation work for 2 years from the date of completion. This covers any issues arising from faulty installation.

6.3 Warranty Exclusions

Warranties do not cover:

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage caused by misuse or accidents
  • Unauthorized modifications or repairs
  • Damage from extreme weather conditions
  • Fading due to sun exposure (normal occurrence)

7.1 Cancellation Policy

  • Orders may be cancelled within 48 hours without penalty
