Understanding Ware's Riverside Heritage

Ware's unique position as one of England's earliest navigation centers has created architectural character unlike anywhere else in Hertfordshire. Our extensive experience working with riverside properties and historic buildings means we understand the specific challenges and opportunities that Ware's distinctive environment presents.

Medieval Town Centre and Ancient Origins

The historic core of Ware, centered around the High Street and Priory Street, retains the pattern of the medieval market town that grew up around the crucial river crossing. Properties dating from the 15th century onwards reflect Ware's importance as a staging post on the Great North Road and a center for the malting trade that would define the town for centuries.

These historic buildings often feature the irregular window openings, thick walls, and structural timber that require careful consideration when installing modern window treatments. We specialize in working within the constraints of ancient buildings, using traditional fixing methods and appropriate materials that respect the building's age while providing contemporary comfort.

Georgian Prosperity and River Navigation

The 18th century brought unprecedented prosperity to Ware through river navigation and the malting industry. This wealth funded the magnificent Georgian buildings that line the High Street and create the elegant residential areas around Church Street and Baldock Street. These properties showcase the period's love of proportion and quality, featuring the perfectly balanced sash windows that are ideal for traditional shutters.

Georgian Ware demonstrates architecture at its most refined, with buildings that were designed to impress and built to last. The generous window proportions and substantial construction provide perfect conditions for full-height plantation shutters that enhance the classical elegance these buildings deserve.

Ware's unique collection of riverside properties, from converted maltings to Georgian houses with river frontages, present specific challenges and opportunities. We've developed specialist expertise in riverside installations, using moisture-resistant materials and weatherproofing techniques that ensure longevity in challenging waterside environments.
